Located in the beautiful campus of one of the most prestigious institutions of India, the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT KGP), the IIT Kharagpur Guest Houses consisting of the Technology Guest House(TGH),Ashutosh Mukherjee Guest House(AM) and Alumni Guest House(VGH), offering AC/ Rooms, Non AC Rooms and Suite Rooms including dinning facilities provides a pleasant accommodation for the Institute’s guests, whether guests from academia, or the Institue's alumni, or the parents/wards of its students. Just a short stroll away from the Main Gate, the primary entry to the institute, these restful residences are flanked by green lawns and walkways. Located inside the campus of Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, the Guest Houses are about 150 KM from Kolkata and about 5 KM from Kharagpur Railway Station. IIT Kharagpur also has a Guest House at Salt Lake – Kolkata, located in Sector – III which is about 20 KM from Airport.
